My path

I studied the Art and Design degree at Escola Massana in Barcelona. As I was finishing my studies, I participated in several digital prototyping projects from Ilaro.org. One of these projects would later become the basis on which Coudre Studio was born: a parametrical modeling interface that allowed us to generate paths, which fed directly to a custom clay 3D printer would produce shapes unlike anything done via traditional 3D printing workflows.

From Coudre Studio I have worked in the design, programming and production of our singular ceramic pieces, as well as taken part in several talks, exhibitions, classes and design research grants. You can know more about these projects over at the Coudre Studio website.

2021-2022
