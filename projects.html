Orniteco

Website Repository

Interactive quiz to learn to identify birds by their song. The birds selected are common to the urban and semi-urban areas of Catalonia, specially of Barcelona.

The audios are provided by the collaborative recording repository xeno-canto and are fetched through the free xeno-canto API.

This website does not use any cookies or collect any data.

See its source at the Github repository.
